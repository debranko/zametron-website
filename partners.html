Founding Partner

Wave AVI

Our founding partner and the single most important reason Zametron exists. We met Wave AVI while working aboard mega yachts, and their mentorship, expertise, and belief in our vision made this company possible.

Wave AVI is a pioneering AV integration firm with offices in London, Netherlands, Phuket, and Singapore. Through Wave AVI, Zametron accesses globally certified engineering capability, manufacturer relationships, and technical support infrastructure.

Connectivity Partner

MTN Satellite Communications

The backbone of our maritime internet solutions. MTN is a leading global satellite and hybrid connectivity provider serving the maritime, yachting, offshore, and corporate sectors.

MTN combines Starlink (LEO), traditional VSAT, OneWeb, and 4G/5G networks with intelligent network management that automatically selects the fastest and most stable connection. Their advanced platforms optimize bandwidth, maintain connectivity continuity, and guarantee secure data transmission — ensuring vessels stay connected anywhere in the world.

Technology Partner

OBEDIO

The smart crew call and butler call system designed for superyachts, luxury villas, and private estates. OBEDIO replaces outdated paging systems with an intelligent voice-to-text service button.

OBEDIO features offline voice-to-text in 100+ languages, ~2 year battery life, and seamless integration with Crestron and Control4 automation systems. Built by crew, for crew, OBEDIO transforms how guests and staff communicate — making service requests instant, clear, and trackable. As a technology partner, OBEDIO completes Zametron's smart automation offering for the most discerning yacht owners and estate managers.
