About Zametron

Where marine engineering heritage meets cutting-edge technology integration.

Our Origins

From the Shipyard to the Bridge

Zametron was born from a simple observation: the world's finest yachts deserved technology systems engineered with the same precision as their hulls.

Founded by marine engineers who had spent years working inside shipyards and on board some of the largest yachts in the world, Zametron became Turkey's first corporate AV and IT integration company dedicated to the maritime sector.

Today we serve mega yachts, luxury villas, and special projects across the globe, partnering with the world's best technology brands to deliver systems that are as reliable as they are impressive.

Our Journey

Milestones

2009

Foundation

Zametron established in Istanbul as Turkey's first corporate AV and IT integration company focused on the maritime sector.

2012

First 50m+ Yacht Project

Completed full AV and IT integration for a 55-meter mega yacht, setting new standards for maritime technology systems.

2015

Crestron Certification

Became a certified Crestron dealer and programmer, expanding our automation capabilities to industry-leading levels.

2018

Wave AVI Partnership

Formed strategic global partnership with Wave AVI, extending our service network across all major yachting hubs worldwide.

2021

Luxury Villa Division

Expanded service offerings to include high-end residential projects, bringing maritime-grade technology to luxury villas.

2024

50+ Yacht Milestone
