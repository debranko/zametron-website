Our Story

About Zametron

Turkey's first corporate AV and IT integration company - born at sea, built for the world.

Vision

Turkey's First Corporate AV & IT Firm

Zametron exists to fill a gap that should not have existed: there was no corporate-level professional AV and IT solutions provider in Turkey. Individuals were doing this work, but no firm had built it into a proper company with the standards and accountability that demanding clients deserve.

We are the first. We carry that distinction with pride and responsibility.

Our goal is to represent Turkey internationally in the AV and IT sector - delivering work that stands alongside the best firms in the world.

What Drives Us

The Founders

From the Sea to the Industry

Both founders are marine engineers - ship mechanical engineers who spent years aboard mega yachts and commercial vessels. Their combined AV and IT experience accumulated during those years at sea led them to create Turkey's first corporate AV and IT firm.

Founding Partner

CEO & Chief Engineer

Co-Founder
